Street Fighter 6 Quick Reference

A simple overview of each character that can fit on a single screen.

The general idea behind this app is to mimic the move list bezels on old arcade machines, or the cards with command lists that came with some versions of Street Fighter IV, V, & other fighting games.

Check out the FAT app for much more robust move information, including frame data.

The Official Street Fighter 6 Website also has move information, including videos for each move.
